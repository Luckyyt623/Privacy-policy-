Privacy Policy

Slither.io Enhanced Controls Browser Extension

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Slither.io Enhanced Controls browser extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data We Collect

The extension collects the following types of information:

  • Chat Messages: Text messages voluntarily sent by users in the real-time chat feature
  • User Interactions: Mouse movements, touch gestures, and click data for game control functionality
  • Game Interface Data: Information needed to modify and enhance the Slither.io gaming experience
  • User Preferences: Settings and configurations chosen by the user

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide real-time chat functionality between players
  • To enable custom cursor controls and touch interface features
  • To enhance the gaming experience with additional controls and UI elements
  • To remember your preferences and settings between sessions
  • To improve the extension's performance and functionality
Important: Chat messages are transmitted in real-time to other players and are not permanently stored on our servers. User settings are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All user preferences, settings, and configurations are stored locally on your device using browser storage
  • Chat Messages: Transient real-time transmission only - not stored long-term
  • Security: We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data
  • Retention: Local data persists until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension

Third-Party Services

The extension uses the following third-party services:

  • WebSocket Servers: For real-time chat functionality (messages are not stored)
  • GitHub: To load static resources like images and icons

These services may have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You can disable chat features at any time
  • You can clear all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • You can choose not to use personalized features
  • You can contact us with privacy concerns (see below)

What We Don't Do

  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes
  • We do NOT track your browsing history outside of Slither.io
  • We do NOT collect financial or payment information
  • We do NOT collect passwords or authentication credentials
  • We do NOT use data for creditworthiness assessment

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer contact form.

Last Updated: October 2025

We may update this policy from time to time. Please check back periodically for changes.
